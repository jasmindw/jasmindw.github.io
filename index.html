About Jasmin

Hello! I am Jasmin De Witte!

My creativity took me to the Arteveldehogeschool in Ghent where I studied crossmedia-design. There, I studied various aspects of design. My favorites were typography and webdesign.

I had an internship at The Reference in Ghent for 3 month as a Graphic designer. I created banners, websites, apps and many more.

In the future, I want to open out myself as a Deviner (combination of designer and develper). Ultimately, I realised that I always need to keep on learning. So in my spare time I learn some programming languages.

At the moment I work for Cartamundi digital as Graphic Designer.
